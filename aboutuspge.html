About Us

Our passion for 3D art

Ever since I was a kid, I have been involved in video games as my whole family are fans of video games. Starting from, like most of us, Minecraft, my most fond childhood memories were hopping onto my brother's computer after a long day of school. Video games have always been a way of escapism for me and as I grew older, I branched out to different genres of videogames, like fantasy rpgs. Even though my favourite games are probably Elden Ring or Baldur's Gate 3, Minecraft will always have a place in my heart.

The older I got, the more I started looking at the artistic aspect of our beloved games. I started researching and experimenting on beginner software, but as I spent more time, I realised it is what I wanted to do for my career. It gave me a way to show my artistic skills like I had always wanted, but 3D fascinated me more than paper.
